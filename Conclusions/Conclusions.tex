\epigraph{``Relative to the potential structural information procurable, it is fair to say that the quantity and quality of the results are a direct reflection of the pains  that have been exercised in measuring and processing the experimental intensities.''\par\raggedleft--- \textup{\textcite[p. 853]{Klug1974c}}, \citetitle{Klug1974c}}



In contrast to giving striking new 'insights' into the atomic arrangement of nano-materials, this dissertation is critically assessing the PDF methodology. This means: investigating effects caused by measurement artefacts, data "correction" and applications of PDF analysis where it is not valid according to the theory.
